📋 How to Play

  1. One person creates a game and shares the 4-letter room code with everyone in the car.
  2. Everyone else joins with the code on their own phone.
  3. Each round shows a Would You Rather question on every device simultaneously.
  4. Everyone picks their answer secretly on their own screen.
  5. When all players have answered, results are revealed!
  6. Players who picked the minority answer (least popular choice) earn +1 point. 🏆
  7. After 10 questions, the leaderboard shows the winner!
